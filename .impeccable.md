## Design Context

### Users
Store owners, their employees, super admins, and their employees. They use this dashboard to actively handle their store operations, while super admins use it to oversee overall business health and activity.

### Brand Personality
Luxury, interesting, trusted. The interface should inspire confidence and efficiency.

### Aesthetic Direction
### Aesthetic Direction
Keep the current UI structure but enhance it.
Visual tone: Sharp (brutalist/modern), keeping the strict sharp corners (`rounded-none`).
Theme: Balanced (equally optimized for Light Mode and Dark Mode). 
Backgrounds must be adapted to elegantly support the warm `#d97757` primary tone (e.g., using warm neutral slates, off-whites like `#fcfaf9` for light mode, and deep warm grays/charcoals like `#1a1614` for dark mode instead of cool blues).

### Design Principles
1. **Sharp Architecture**: Maintain completely sharp corners (`rounded-none`) across all elements for a modern, uncompromising aesthetic.
2. **Luxury & Trust**: Use typography and spacing to create a high-end feel. Enhance the current glassmorphism and depth carefully without making it look cheap.
3. **Straight to the Point**: Function over decorative clutter. The layout should be extremely efficient, maximizing readability for store owners overseeing data.
4. **Balanced Contrast**: Ensure both light and dark modes provide an equally premium and readable experience. Integrate the new refined primary color (`#d97757`) gracefully against tailored warm-neutral background palettes for both themes.
